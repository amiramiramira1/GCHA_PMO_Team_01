## Conflict Resolution Process

When a disagreement arises, each team member will share their viewpoint clearly.
The team will discuss the options and choose the one that best meets the project goals, with the current Session Team Leader guiding the discussion.
If the team is unsure or the decision could affect the project, we will ask the instructor for help.
